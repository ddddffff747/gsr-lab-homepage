Welcome to GSR Lab

The Geo System Research Laboratory at Hanyang University focuses on advanced computational methods to solve challenging geotechnical engineering problems. Our primary interests include geotechnical earthquake engineering, soil-structure interaction, and numerical analysis of underground structures.

We specialize in seismic analysis of underground structures, liquefaction assessment, seismic slope stability, and the development of innovative numerical simulation tools.

Research Areas

Seismic Performance of Underground Structures

Damage analysis of tunnels, fragility curves, machine learning-based seismic performance prediction

Seismic Site Amplification & Ground Response Analysis

Equivalent-linear and nonlinear ground response analysis for Korean geological environment

Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

Deep learning for site amplification prediction, AI-driven seismic analysis

Probabilistic Seismic Hazard Analysis

Site-specific PSHA, seismic hazard curves, uniform hazard response spectra

Liquefaction Assessment

Physical and numerical procedures for liquefaction triggering and consequence evaluation

Seismic Response of Slopes

Newmark displacement estimation, dynamic slope stability analysis

High-Fidelity Blast Simulation

Blast-induced vibration propagation, fracture zone prediction

Tall Building – Basement Interaction

Seismic earth pressure on embedded structures, soil-structure interaction

Offshore Engineering

Bucket foundation design, spudcan penetration, offshore wind turbine foundations
