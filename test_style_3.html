About the Laboratory

The Geo System Research Laboratory at Hanyang University focuses on advanced computational methods to solve challenging geotechnical engineering problems. Led by Professor Duhee Park (박두희), our primary interests include geotechnical earthquake engineering, soil-structure interaction, and numerical analysis of underground structures.

Research Areas

01Site Response

Earthquake-Soil Interaction

Seismic wave propagation, site response analysis, and soil amplification characteristics.

02Hazard

Seismic Ground Failure & Hazard Assessment

Liquefaction, slope stability, and probabilistic hazard frameworks.

03SSI

Seismic Behavior of Underground Structures

Tunnels, metro systems, retaining walls, and foundations with SSI.

04FEM

Numerical Simulation & Predictive Modeling

Nonlinear FEM analysis and predictive models for seismic performance.

05Deep Learning

AI & Machine Learning

Physics-informed deep learning for seismic response prediction and automated characterization.
