Join Our Lab

We are always looking for motivated students and researchers who are interested in geotechnical earthquake engineering and soil-structure interaction. If you are passionate about computational geomechanics and want to contribute to innovative research, we encourage you to apply.

Graduate Students (M.S. / Ph.D.)

We accept applications for graduate programs every semester. Applicants should have a background in civil engineering, geotechnical engineering, or related fields. Competitive scholarships are available for outstanding candidates.

Postdoctoral Researchers

Positions are available for postdoctoral researchers with expertise in numerical methods, geotechnical earthquake engineering, seismic hazard analysis, or soil dynamics.

Visiting Researchers

We welcome visiting researchers and scholars for collaborative research opportunities. Exchange programs with international universities are available.

To apply or inquire about positions, please send your CV and a brief statement of research interests to dpark@hanyang.ac.kr
